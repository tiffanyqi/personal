Tiffany's Book List

Books are awesome. Here is a compilation of 84 books that are on my personal book list, which are from reputable sources on Product Management, Self Improvement, and Modern China. The great thing about PM books is that they encompass many different areas such as content, design, leadership, psychology, and technology! So it's a pretty good selection that I still swear by. I started since June 2016, and I still have a long way to go!

Green means I've completed the book, and gray means I gave up reading it. Hopefully I can go back in a couple years and try again. Working on an article to recommend books that I've read :)
