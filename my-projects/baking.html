17. Corgi butt vanilla buttercream cake (04.08.18)
One of my coworkers love corgis, and through a recommendation of another friend, decided to make a vanilla buttercream cake with swiss buttercream in the shape of a corgi butt. I was really anxious at first, but I now have a newfound love of piping, and I'll be definitely piping more cakes in the future.Recipe.
16. Vanilla sponge cake roll (04.06.18)
Decided to make the same vanilla sponge cake roll again, made a whipping cream without heavy cream but messed up on the recipe, so I made it without filling. The top also came out not as pretty, but still tasted good. Recipe.
15. Portuguese egg tarts (04.01.18)
Made with pre-made puff pastry, turned out well aside from the fact that since I used half of the original recipe, the log turned out thinner than expected. As a result, it was much harder to get the dough up the side of the tin. Definitely will try this again. Recipe.
14. Rice pudding (03.31.18)
Made this as a spontaneous treat. Apparently forgot to take pictures, but the taste was great, the texture was a bit tougher than creamy but still good. Recipe.
13. King cake (03.30.18)
This was the first time kneading bread really, which is how you make a king cake. This recipe had the dough very oily, and I didn't realize to add more flour. This sugary glaze bread is what happened. Recipe.
12. Cream puffs (03.27.18)
Forgot to take a picture, but these were more consistent in size like the one in this picture. I brought empty shells to work, and then piped whipped cream during lunchtime. Since the shells were made a day before, it wasn't as crispy as when it was freshly made. Recipe.
11. Viennese whirls (03.25.18)
Inspired by the Great British Bake Off. Unfortunately, the butter was not soft enough for the biscuits and so they were rolled rather than piped. Still quite tasty! Recipe.
10. Choux cake (03.24.18)
Poured the same cream puff batter from last week into a single pan, just to see what would happen. Wasn't baked for nearly long enough, and I added an extra half tsp of salt, so it turned out not as expected. Pretty with powdered sugar. Recipe.
9. Cream puffs (03.18.18)
Cream puffs turn out to be easier than I expected! Tasty when served immediately. Not at all good when stuck in the freezer overnight. Recipe.
8. Double chocolate loaf (03.17.18)
Tackled a double chocolate loaf: chocolate cake with a chocolate ganache. Dense, but also a bit moist as well. Chocolate cake recipe and chocolate ganache recipe.
7. Japanese cheesecake (03.11.18)
The same day, I also wanted to try the infamous 3 ingredient Japanese cheesecake. I think the cream cheese wasn't soft enough and the white chocolate hardened slightly, which gave it a slightly uneven texture. Flavor was spot on, and was better after chilling overnight in the fridge. Recipe.
6. Chocolate meringue pie (03.11.18)
Lemon? What about chocolate? Turned out alright. Still learning about cooling and storage to prevent droplets from forming. Recipe.
5. Lemon meringue pie (03.04.18)
For the first time, I decide to not make a cake roll this week and go for another GBBO challenge: Lemon meringue pie. Learned that to prevent the meringue from weeping, you have to place it when it's hot and chill the pie for a couple of hours. Turned alright other than that. Recipe.
4. Vanilla sponge cake roll (02.25.18)
Finally, I decide to go with a separate recipe, this time a more Asian flair to it using an egg separation trick. So much better, also managed to keep the tops intact. Recipe.
3. Vanilla sponge roulade (02.24.18)
I want to redeem myself, so I tried again. I used the same recipe as Bake #2, and the batter was tougher than the previous week, and cracked a lot. Recipe, without the strawberries, sprinkled with powdered sugar.
2. Strawberries and cream sponge roulade (02.18.18)
Annoyed about the previous day's fail, I decide to try again with a sponge. It turns out a bit better, and the whipped cream was good. Recipe.
1. Strawberries and cream meringue roulade (02.17.18)
So many things went wrong with this. First, this is a meringue roulade, which is 10x more difficult than a sponge roulade and I was just getting back into it. Second, I didn't realize that the heavy cream should be whipped (rookie mistake), which turned into this disaster.
