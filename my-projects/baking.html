18. Pomelo meringue pie (04.14.18)
I wanted to try a variation of lemon meringue pie using pomelos. In my experience, every time I've baked in the oven, it turned out wheepy, so I wanted instead to chill the curd, pipe swiss meringue (which is firmer than french), and broil/blowtorch it real quick. This was the result! Some of the tips became black, but I know now to pipe evenly. Pie recipe, swiss meringue recipe.
