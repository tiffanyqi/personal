About

At Mixpanel, we were so sad that our favorite cookie delivery company went out of business. In July 2018, I set out on a mission to create cookies that were just as delicious, more convenient and at a fraction of the price. Fellow coworkers and friends A/B tested the recipes, and finally after much trial and error, they were ready.

How it works

Getting cookies to you is simple. Order online, select the cookies of your choice, and that morning cookies will arrive at your desk!

Use Mixbies for celebrations, catering, QA sessions, tech talks, or whenever you just want a cookie.

Please note that I accept deliveries for Monday, Wednesday, and Friday, and do not do same day deliveries at this time.
